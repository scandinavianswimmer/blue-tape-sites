# Local SEO notes for Blue Tape Sites

Google Business Profile guidance confirms that service-area businesses such as plumbers and cleaners should define service areas by specific cities or postal areas rather than a radius, and that the overall area should generally stay within about two hours of driving time from the business base. It also notes that service-area businesses that do not serve customers at their address should remove the address from the profile and rely on service areas instead.

Search Engine Land's service-area page guidance emphasizes that location visibility improves when pages clearly reference the city and service in titles, headings, meta descriptions, internal links, and useful page copy. It warns against thin, duplicative city pages and recommends building genuinely helpful, unique service-area content, strong contact and CTA visibility, FAQs, and crawlable internal linking to location pages or a cities-served hub.

Implementation direction for this site:

1. Strengthen homepage metadata and on-page copy around Southern California service-area keywords.
2. Add visible references to core service cities/areas on the page rather than leaving geography generic.
3. Add structured data for LocalBusiness / ProfessionalService with service-area details.
4. Prepare the site architecture so city-specific service-area pages can be added next without doorway-page duplication.
5. Ensure the contact form truly stores leads and surfaces a successful submission state.

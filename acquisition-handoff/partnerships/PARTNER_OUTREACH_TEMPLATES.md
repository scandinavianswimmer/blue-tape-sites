# Partner Outreach Templates

**Purpose:** Give Blue Tape Sites ready-to-send starting points for outreach to adjacent professionals who already serve contractors.

## How to Use These Templates

These templates are meant to be customized lightly, not blasted unchanged. Each note should mention the partner’s actual line of work and make it clear that Blue Tape Sites is offering a complementary service, not trying to hijack the relationship.

The tone should remain direct, professional, and low-pressure. The best partner outreach sounds like a credible collaboration note, not like aggressive business development.

## Template 1: First Introduction Email

> **Subject:** Possible fit for your contractor clients  
> Hi [Name], I’m reaching out because you already work with the kind of contractor businesses Blue Tape Sites is built for. We help companies whose websites feel weaker than the actual business — especially when trust, clarity, and presentation are costing them calls or making them look less established online than they really are.  
>  
> I thought there might be a natural fit if you ever have clients who say their site feels outdated, generic, or not as credible as the work they do. If helpful, I can send over a very short summary of the exact kinds of companies we’re best for and the situations where we tend to help most.

## Template 2: Shorter DM / Text Version

> Hi [Name] — quick note because you already work with contractors. Blue Tape Sites helps home-service companies whose websites feel weaker than the business itself. If you ever have a client saying their site feels dated, generic, or low-trust, I’d be glad to be a useful referral option.

## Template 3: Follow-Up After No Reply

> **Subject:** Re: possible fit for your contractor clients  
> Hi [Name], just following up in case this is relevant later. Blue Tape Sites is the strongest fit when a contractor already has a real business but the website is not carrying the same level of trust or professionalism online. If that kind of situation comes up in your client base, I’m happy to make the handoff process simple.

## Template 4: After a Positive Reply

> Thanks — I appreciate it. To make it easy, the best fit is usually a contractor business that already does solid work but feels undersold online. Right now we’re especially focused on plumbers and remodelers. If it helps, I can send a one-paragraph intro you can forward when someone relevant comes to mind.

## Template 5: Forwardable Intro for the Partner to Send

> I wanted to connect you with Blue Tape Sites. They work with contractors whose websites feel weaker than the quality of the actual business. If you’ve been meaning to improve trust, presentation, or clarity online, they’re worth a conversation.

## Template 6: Thank-You After a Referral

> Thanks for making the intro — I appreciate it. I’ll keep the next step clear and straightforward from here. If I can ever return the favor or send work your way when it fits, I’d be glad to.

## Talking Points for Live Conversation

If the conversation moves to a call, Blue Tape Sites should keep the framing simple.

| Talking Point | What to Emphasize |
|---|---|
| **What Blue Tape Sites does** | Helps contractors improve trust, clarity, presentation, and conversion through better websites |
| **Who fits best** | Serious operators whose site undersells the business |
| **Why partners benefit** | Their clients look better, convert better, and get a cleaner next step |
| **How referrals work** | The handoff is easy and Blue Tape Sites takes over cleanly after the intro |

## Partner-Specific Personalization Notes

| Partner Type | Angle to Mention |
|---|---|
| **Photographer / videographer** | Better websites make strong visual work pay off harder |
| **Copywriter / brand strategist** | Better website execution protects and sharpens good messaging work |
| **SEO consultant** | Better trust and conversion help the traffic they drive perform better |
| **Business coach** | Better presentation supports the broader business upgrade they’re advising |
| **CRM / automation consultant** | Better top-of-funnel quality improves the leads their systems handle |

## Final Reminder

The goal is to create a few real, high-quality partner relationships, not a giant list of cold contacts. Blue Tape Sites should favor relevance and trust over volume.

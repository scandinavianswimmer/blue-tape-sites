# Blue Tape Sites Referral One-Pager

**For partner use**

## What Blue Tape Sites Does

Blue Tape Sites designs websites for serious home-service businesses that need to look as credible online as they are in real life. The focus is on companies whose current websites undersell the quality of their work, weaken trust, or make the next step feel unclear.

This is not commodity “marketing help.” The work is centered on clearer positioning, stronger conversion structure, better presentation, and a more trustworthy first impression for contractors who already do solid work but whose websites are holding them back.

## The Best-Fit Client

Blue Tape Sites is the strongest fit for contractor businesses that care about how they are represented, want a sharper online presence, and are willing to invest in looking established rather than cheap.

| Best-Fit Signals | What They Usually Sound Like |
|---|---|
| **Their website feels dated** | “Our business is solid, but the site doesn’t look like us anymore.” |
| **They want better trust online** | “We get referrals, but the website doesn’t help us close.” |
| **They are growing beyond word of mouth** | “We need a site that actually supports the next stage.” |
| **They care about presentation** | “We want to look more professional without feeling generic.” |
| **They are a serious operator** | “We’re not looking for the cheapest option; we want it done right.” |

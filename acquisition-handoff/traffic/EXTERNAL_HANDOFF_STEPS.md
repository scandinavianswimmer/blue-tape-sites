# External Handoff Steps for Blue Tape Sites Traffic Execution

The landing pages and internal planning files are ready. The remaining work below depends on platform logins, verification, or business details that should come from the owner side.

## 1. Business Details to Finalize Before Any Profile Claims

| Needed detail | Why it matters | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Public phone number** | Must stay identical across Google, Apple, Bing, Yelp, and directory profiles | Owner to confirm |
| **Public contact email** | Needed for verifications and follow-up contact | Owner to confirm |
| **Exact service-area phrasing** | Should be consistent across directories and profiles | Owner to confirm |
| **Business hours** | Affects profile completeness and trust | Owner to confirm |
| **Logo and cover image** | Required for company pages and richer profile presentation | Owner to confirm |

## 2. Platforms That Require Owner Login or Verification

| Platform | Likely owner action needed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Business Profile** | Google login, business verification, profile completion | Use `FIRST_10_PROFILE_CLAIMS.csv` as the checklist |
| **Apple Business Connect** | Apple login and business verification | Keep the same website, phone, and service-area wording |
| **Bing Places** | Microsoft login and listing verification | Can often import from Google after Google is completed |
| **LinkedIn Company Page** | LinkedIn account access | Founder profile and company page should link to each other |
| **Yelp / BBB / Manta / Foursquare** | Account creation or claim verification | Complete only with consistent business details |

## 3. Outreach Steps That Should Be Sent From a Real Owner or Company Inbox

| Outreach type | Recommended sender | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Partner intro emails** | Founder or company email | Higher trust and better reply rates |
| **Resource-page mention requests** | Founder or company email | Looks more legitimate than a cold alias |
| **Podcast or publication pitches** | Founder or company email | Makes follow-up easier if accepted |

## 4. Recommended Order of Operations

First, lock the business details in `PROFILE_CLAIM_PREP.md`. Second, complete the first three profiles: Google Business Profile, Apple Business Connect, and Bing Places. Third, finish LinkedIn company and founder profile cleanup. Fourth, personalize the first outreach batch from `FIRST_15_PARTNER_OUTREACH_BATCH.csv` and send it from a real inbox.

## 5. What the Next Agent Can Do Without Owner Login

The next run can continue building the live site, refine the landing pages further, enrich the outreach target sheet with named prospects, draft custom messages, and prepare copy for listings. The next run should not submit external profiles or send live outreach without the owner side details above.

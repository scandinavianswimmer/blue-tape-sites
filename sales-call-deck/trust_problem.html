Problem framing

Most contractor sites lose trust before the call.

The business may be solid, but the website can still make it feel smaller, less established, or harder to trust than it really is.
02
Traffic does not fix a weak first impression. If the page creates hesitation, even a strong service company will lose momentum before the visitor reaches out.
Weak headlines
Visitors cannot tell quickly what the company does, where it works, or why it is the safer choice.
Thin proof
The business may have real credibility, but the page does not show enough proof to justify the next click or call.
Confusing offers
If people have to decode the service, pricing range, or next step, many of them leave before taking action.

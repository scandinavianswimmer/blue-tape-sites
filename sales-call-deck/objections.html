Common clarity points

Direct answers to the questions you may already have.

10
Most hesitation is simple. You want to know whether you need a rebuild, whether the price range is real, and what happens after the audit.
Clarity 01
Do you need a full rebuild?
Not always. If the bones are usable, the right fixes can still sharpen trust, improve clarity, and move the site forward without starting from scratch.
Clarity 02
Why show pricing publicly?
Because you should know the ballpark before you give up time for a call. It keeps the conversation honest and helps you decide faster whether the fit is real.
Clarity 03
What happens after the audit?
You get a marked-up review, direct recommendations, and a clearer next-step conversation instead of being pushed straight into a vague proposal.
